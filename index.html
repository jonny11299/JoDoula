Artwork by Bella Rivera

I'm Jo Bechtold, a Post-Partum Doula.

A Post-Partum Doula is someone who cares for a newborn baby and their family in the immediate weeks after birth.

Doulas provide newborn care guidance, emotional and physical support, resources and information to help you through this transformative era of your life. To learn more about my services, click here!

Please use this website as a guide to learn more about me and my services.

***

I love being a Post-Partum Doulas because it makes me feel connected to my ancestors. I feel connected to my Divine Feminine energy - the time I spend with new families as a woman is so valuable and essential. This work reminds me of a time when there was one woman in the village who sat with new families and mothered the new mother. Being part of this ancient tradition makes me feel a part of our connected liberations. It's what I was put on this earth to do.
